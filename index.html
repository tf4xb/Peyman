Company

Novex, Makers of the Nation's Best Tissue and Paper Products Since 2000

Novex Products Inc., is a growing manufacturer of tissue, towel, and napkin products. We operate from multiple plant sites in the United States. Our products are designed for applications in the away-from-home market as well as retail consumptions. In addition to our standard items, we also manufacture custom made paper products per customer specifications. We use earth friendly materials and components in our products. We manufacture a wide range of products and strive to be a one-stop shop to all distributors of janitorial, food service, and paper products. Our products are consistent, elegant, cost effective, and eco-friendly.

Eco-Conscience

Eco-Conscience, Our commitment

Novex Products is an environmentally conscientious organization. In our manufacturing operations, we recycle all excess cardboard, paper, tissue, plastic and cores. Many of our products including dispenser roll towels, napkins and toilet tissues are manufactured with 100% recycled paper made in the United States. Additionally, we have unique and exciting top-quality products made with Kraft unbleached materials. Our Kraft Jumbo Kitchen Towel T7300 is simply one of the best towels manufactured in the US. And our Two-Ply Kraft Dinner Napkin, N1600 is the best Kraft Two-Ply product in the market, where elegance and environmental commitments are merged into making a beautiful and functional product.
